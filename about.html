I’m David, a data analyst with a background in accounting and auditing, based in Hamburg, Germany. I transitioned into data analytics after several years in the public sector, where I worked with large datasets in financial, performance, and compliance audits. I’ve since developed my skills in data cleaning, analysis, SQL, Python, and storytelling through visualization. My approach is rooted in curiosity, clarity, and creating value from data. Outside of analytics, I’m a lifelong learner who enjoys simplifying complexity and exploring new ways to communicate insights effectively.
