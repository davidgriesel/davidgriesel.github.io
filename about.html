I’m a data analyst with a background in accounting and auditing that started out supporting small businesses with financial reporting and analysis, before moving into public sector audit.

Being responsible for processing and maintaining financial databases gave me a solid understanding of how information is structured, processed, and used in decision-making.

Later, when I worked with large and complex datasets, I applied this knowledge to enhance audit processes by expanding the use of data analytics and computer-assisted audit techniques (CAATs).

Over time, this led to a dedicated role in data analytics, where I introduced custom scripts, eliminated repetitive tasks, optimising workflows, and improving overall efficiency.

More recently, I’ve been building on this foundation by developing my skills in coding, statistics, advanced analytics, and data visualisation.

Today, I apply these principles and skills to data analytics projects — uncovering insights, solving challenges, and exploring new tools along the way.
